Our Mission

FC Barcelona Juniors is a fun-first academy for players ages 6-12. We develop confident players by blending Barca-inspired technical training with a family atmosphere that celebrates every child.

Teamwork

We teach kids to move the ball, communicate, and support one another like their Barca heroes.

Small-sided rondos and cooperative challenges build shared habits and leadership from day one.

Creativity

Free play, bold ideas, and expression on the field help every player find their flair.

We mix skill labs with freestyle labs so kids feel brave experimenting with skills that make the crowd cheer.

Respect

We model empathy, sportsmanship, and fair play with opponents, referees, and teammates.

Mindset huddles and parent workshops help the entire family embrace the Barca values of humility and effort.
